About Seekho Two Wheeler Academy

Empowering Independence Through Safe Riding — trusted scooty & bike training across Kolkata since 2018.

Our Story

Seekho Two Wheeler Academy was founded with a simple belief: everyone deserves the freedom to ride. What started as a small training centre in Kolkata has grown into a multi-branch academy with 5000+ successful students.

What Makes Us Different

Patient, Expert Trainers
Certified instructors who specialize in beginners and non-cyclists.
Women-First Approach
Dedicated ladies batches in a safe, supportive environment.
4 Kolkata Branches
Tollygunge, New Town, Barasat and Sodepur — pick what's nearest.

Our Promise

We don't just teach you to ride — we build lasting confidence. From your first balance drill to guided traffic practice, every lesson is designed to make you an independent, safe rider on Kolkata roads.
